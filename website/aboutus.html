Tips for Fresh flowers

  • Trim the stems:Cutting about an inch off the stems at an angle helps to promote water uptake, and keep your flowers fresh
  • Change the water:Fresh water every 2-3 dayus helps to prevent batcteral growth
  • Avoid direct sunlight: While flowers love sunlights, direct sunlight can cause them to wilt and fade faster
  • Keep them cool:Most flowers prefer cooler temperatures, around 65-75'f to stay fresh
